About Me

Hello Everyone!

My name is Christopher Andrew Kostick and I am a current Senior here at Rutgers University

Major: Information Technology / Minor: Critical Intelligence Studies

Hobbies: I like to go hiking, play video games, and play sports (Futbol, Basketball)

The reason I have the logo as a Red Cardinal is because whenever someone in my family passes we have it to where they visit us through a Red Cardinal symbolizing we are not alone in this world, we will always have each other.

My IT History

I became interested in Information Technology through Professor Messor and his videos on YouTube along with my Cousin who does IT work with Google and has been for multiple years. Seeing him be successful and actually introduce me to the line of work, had made me interested in what career I could build for myself.

My School

I am in my last semester here at Rutgers with a current GPA of 3.75 Majoring in Information Technology along with a Minor in Critical Intelligence Studies. I am a 3 year attendee of the RUSecurity club where we have weekly meetings and discuss the latest Cyber news and complete labs to which can help boost our skills and apply them to our resume and our careers.

Where I Grew Up

Before moving to New Jersey I had lived in a place called Staten Island, Tottenville to be specific. It is said to be the "little brother" or the "forgotten borough" but I dont think of it that way. I think of it as the place my life really started, especially my high school. Made most of my long term friendships there, met some amazing teachers, and still kept connections there because now my cousins who stayed behind are just starting high school this year.
